01

Site Name

RPG Realm

The name RPG Realm captures the essence of the site in two words. "RPG" immediately signals the genre — role-playing games — while "Realm" evokes the fantasy worlds these games are set in. It is short, memorable, and easy to brand.

Optional domain: rpg-realm.com

02

Site Purpose

RPG Realm is an informational guide for players who are new to or curious about role-playing games. The site provides:

  • A curated, filterable showcase of popular RPG and fantasy games with ratings, platforms, and genre tags.
  • A character classes guide explaining the most common RPG archetypes (warrior, mage, rogue, etc.) with descriptions and playstyle tips.
  • An interactive quiz that helps visitors discover which RPG character class best fits their personality, with results saved locally.
